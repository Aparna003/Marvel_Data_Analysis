Marvel's Cinematic Journey: From Unstoppable Force to
Waning Power

Marvel's cinematic dominance reached an all-time high in 2019 with an extraordinary $5 billion global box office haul, driven by the success of Avengers: Endgame. However, the franchise faced significant declines in the following years, influenced by the COVID-19 pandemic, shifts in audience preferences, and franchise fatigue, with revenues dropping to $1309 million by 2023...
